Main Headline

“Your fleet inventory, finally under control.”

Manage uniforms, onboard linens, equipment, and crews from a single app. From the captain to the shipowner, everyone has the right access and the right information at the right time.

Managing a yacht’s inventory is a constant headache.

Yacht crews face the same challenges every season:

  • 🗂️ Missing uniforms: Who took what? What size? What condition?
  • 📋 Excel spreadsheets scattered among the captain, the head steward, and the owner
  • 𔔄 Frequent crew rotations that make tracking impossible
  • 💸 Financial losses due to lost, damaged, or double-ordered items
  • 📦 No visibility into actual stock across the marina, the yacht, and the fleet

The Solution

Marina Yacht Inventory centralizes all your inventory and crew management on a single platform, accessible anywhere, in real time.

3 Key Features

Real-time Inventory

Track every item—uniforms, accessories, linens—by yacht, category, and condition. No more losses, duplicates, or unpleasant surprises at the start of the season.

Integrated Crew Management

Assign items directly to your crew members. Manage sizes, initial issue lists, and assignment history with just a few clicks.

Alerts & Expense Tracking

Receive automatic alerts for low stock or worn-out items. Track every expense related to your inventory for transparent accounting.
